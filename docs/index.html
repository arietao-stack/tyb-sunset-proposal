04 / 07
Diagnosis

Why it doesn't work for us

1 · Shallow by design

Taps, not actions

Polls get ~273 answers; uploads get ~5. The deep actions that create traffic and sales barely happen.

2 · Growth stopped

No new buyers

+805 in May → +33 in half of July. TYB doesn't bring anyone new to us.

3 · Wrong stage

Built for big fandoms

The brands that win on TYB arrive famous. We're still building awareness.

4 · Support is tiered

Platinum brands first

Replies are slow and hands-on help goes to TYB's Platinum/Gold tier. $1,333/mo doesn't buy us that.

Our cadence stayed high — the response didn't

101 challenges since April — near-daily posting from our side, with referral rewards attached. Effort isn't the gap; depth of member action is.

Execution

The 3-month wind-down, fixed by contract

1
Now · week of Jul 20

Serve written cancellation notice

Confirm the end date (≈Oct 18) and the exact obligations. Ask for early release or a lighter cadence in the same letter — any yes is a bonus.

2
Aug – Oct · continuous

Run the 2/day obligation cheaply

Build a challenge template library once, schedule in bulk. VA/EA runs it — a few hours a month, no new creative spend. Targets set at floor levels.

3
Sep · Sep 15 – Oct 10

Move members to our own channels

Announce warmly, give a final coin-redemption window, invite everyone to email/SMS with 15% off. Personal message to top contributors → creator program.

4
Contract end · Oct 18 – 31

Close, export, document

Community closes, data exported, learnings written up in one page.

Why not wait or renegotiate? Waiting adds $1,333 + a month of work per month of delay. A discount buys a cheaper version of the same result. Community gets revisited when the brand has a real fandom.
